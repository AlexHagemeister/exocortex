# staging/ — import holding area

Notes brought from elsewhere, waiting to enter the system. Nothing here is read, swept, or indexed by the pipeline — the maintainer touches this folder only to move a batch out when you direct it.

To import: move batches into `notes/` (your own words — swept for knowledge, never edited) or `sources/inbox/` (external material — validated and ingested). The move is what activates them; until then the files just sit here, synced and searchable. Migration guide: SETUP.md § 9 in the program repo.
